Campaign promises

My campaign is built on the following 3 pillars.

1.
Transparency

Student council can be mysterious. Previous presidents have promised transparency, yet only 1 in 10 surveyed students felt they knew what happened at meetings. I promise to publish meeting notes on a weekly basis through a brand-new SAC website.

2.
Accountability

During my time as secretary/treasurer, I observed many SAC members failing to attend meetings on a regular basis. As the elected and appointed representatives of the school, we can do better. I promise to propose a minimum of 70% meeting attendance for members to keep their position.

3.
Progress

Our school deserves a student council that works on moving forward. I promise to continue proposing new initiatives and working on ongoing projects.
